Wireless Stethoscope using Arduino & HC-05 Bluetooth

15

Stethoscope is one of the most important diagnostic tools in medical world. Over Almost two centuries the stethocope has been changed and refined often,but it has never strayed too far the original design. To replace analog stethoscope which is not efficient for recording the heartbeats with bluetooth stethoscope .And also our project is to simplify the task of checking heartbeats of the subject woth no noise operational amplifier, which neglects the noise of the Heartbeat and records it. The arduino technology used in Bluetooth stethoscope allows for the precise and reliable capture of heart or lung sounds,while the bluetooth coneectivity enables seamless transmission of these sounds to a computer or a mobile device.The device can also be customized and programmed and to meet specific medical needs and preferences making it a versatile and flexible tool for medical professionals. Overall the Arduino based Bluetooth Stethoscope represents a significant advancement in medical technology enabling more accurate and efficient diagnosis and improving patient.

Components Used

1. Arduino UNO
2. HC-05 Bluetooth Module
3. NE5532 Op-Amp - 1
4. Dual Polarity Power Supply
5. 3.3K Resistor 1
6. 470nF Capacitor - 1








Conclusion

It aims at designing an electronic stethoscope .A circuit is design to perform the same function as the conventional stethoscope . Recently,techniques related to advances in electronic medical instrument are developed procedure HS signalalso are analyze and clarify the ensuing sounds, to make diagnosis based on quantifiable medical assesments. This can reduce the tim required compared to amanual reading but can still take time due to a requirement to travel from wheel to wheel measuring the heartbeat. This becomes more important when using the digital stethoscope because of the increased possibility for the display to be corrupted,perhaps from the malfunction or malicious software on a display device.
